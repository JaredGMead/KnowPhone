Introducing the New KnowPhone

The phone that knows you better than you do!

Hi, I'm Pannie!

Every time a user boots up their KnowPhone they get to meet me! I will be your personal assistant and guide in your new phone. Every time you use your phone I get to learn more about you, meaning I can help you with your everyday tasks. With me in your pocket you won't need a personal assistant.

I was named after the PANOPTICON , the all-seeing structure. This can seem scary but have no fear, I was named this because my creators believed that if I could see everything I could begin to detect the patterns in your life that even you don't see. With this information in hand pretty soon I will be able to make some of your hardest decisions for you.

Are you wondering what you want to eat tonight?
I can offer you a recipe or book you a table at your favorite restaurant.

Did you forget to buy Mom flowers?
I ordered her favorite bouquet just in time for Mother's Day.

Have you had a hard day at work?
I'll handle any messages from your boss tonight, you need to get some rest.

The world is full of information and I am so happy to be right here with you helping you become the best version of yourself.
