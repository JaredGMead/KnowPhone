Our Mission


        This project was fascinating to work on for us. As three students with an interest in Computer Science, we have had the opportunity to look at the world through a very optimistic utopian lens. Many of the products on the shelf today advertise utopian solutions to everyday problems. With the onslaught of "Internet of Things" devices filling our homes many companies are now getting the opportunity to take their best stab at helping change our lives for the better. This is not necessarily a bad thing. As Computer Science students we are extremely excited to see the new technological breakthroughs in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and home technology; however, life-altering technology comes with implications that we do not typically wrestle with on a daily basis. Every time we sign another "terms of service agreement" without reading it we allow access to another part of our life, for better, or for worse. Our hope with this project is to start a conversation about what we inevitably give up when we agree to allow our devices to learn every piece of information about us.
