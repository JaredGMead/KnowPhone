Genome Verification

Don't worry about anybody taking a peek at your data again! Simply store your Genome as your security setting and the only person able to access your data other than you is our team of trained data scientists.

Safe Backups

In our current society your data is just as important to you as a limb. The KnowPhone will automatically image your device every night to ensure that you will never be too far away from your data. These images will be stored onsite in one of our award winning data storage facilities. This allows us to keep your information secure for you, as well as allowing our data scientists to analyze your 'data profile' to help improve your daily life.

Need to Dispose of Data?

In the unlikely event that the wrong person gets their hands on your phone the KnowPhone will send one final backup to the central servers before initializing the 'self-destruct' directive. This directive will detonate a small charge stored next to the KnowPhone's storage chip, preventing any of your data from getting in the wrong hands. The Backup will be accesible from any participating stores, allowing for an easy way to help get your life back in order after losing your phone!
